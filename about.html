Our story

Named for the part that comes after the fall.

Founder Karan Bhatia opened Phoenix Physiques in 2020 after watching too many people quit fitness entirely once life knocked them off track — an injury, a job loss, a long gap with no training at all. Most gyms made them feel behind. He wanted to build one that didn't.

Six years later, the gym has grown from a two-room setup to a full floor with strength, conditioning, boxing, and a coaching team built specifically to work with people restarting — not just people who never stopped.

What we stand for

The principles behind every program.

No judgment on your starting point

Whether it's day one or year ten of training, your assessment is honest and your plan starts from where you actually are.

Form before intensity

We'd rather slow your first month down than have an injury take your next six off the table.

Plans that adapt

Life interrupts training. Our coaches adjust your program around setbacks instead of treating them as failure.

Clean, working equipment

Machines are serviced on a fixed schedule — a broken cable shouldn't be the reason you skip a session.
